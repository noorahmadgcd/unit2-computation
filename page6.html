∙ divine scales ∙

Beyond the sky resides an endless realm; its absolute state, components and makeup currently remains unknown to mankind. It is difficult for the human mind to comprehend the vastness of the scale of the universe and everything inside and beyond it. "إِنَّا زَيَّنَّا ٱلسَّمَآءَ ٱلدُّنْيَا بِزِينَةٍ ٱلْكَوَاكِبِ" translates to "Indeed, We have adorned the lowest heaven with the stars for decoration".
